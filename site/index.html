About

I'm a Uruguayan computer scientist and statistician, trained at Universidad de la República in Montevideo (Ingeniería en Computación, 2022), where I'm also finishing a BSc in Statistics. I've published research in distributed deep generative models, and my work sits at the intersection of statistical learning, algorithm design, and high-performance computing.

Alongside research, I've spent 6+ years building production software for United States clients as a Senior Software Engineer at WyeWorks, where I focus on framework and interface design and help shape technical direction. What I do best is turn tangled problems into clean, composable abstractions. I bring that same rigor and craft to research software: statistical methods, implemented to be fast, tested, and reproducible.
